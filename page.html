Sri Lanka is one of the most beautiful countries in the world and people in Sri Lanka live very freely. Both domestic and international visitors can travel freely throughout the nation. Top Sri Lankan tourist sites can be visited in a minimum of 5 - 6 days. It is very simple to visit all of Sri Lanka's best attractions in a single journey. One can start from Colombo and visit Sigiriya, Anuradhapura, Kandy, Nuwara Eliya, Galle, Benthota and nearby places.
In here I am shown four different beautiful places to visit in Sri Lanka.

Places Details

The central Sri Lankan town of Sigiriya, also known as Lion Rock or Lion Mountain, contains the remains of an ancient stronghold that was constructed there in the late 5th century CE on a stunning monolithic rock structure.

The temple of tooth, which is part of the former Kingdom of Kandy's royal palace compound, is where the Buddha's tooth relic is kept. The relic has been significant in local politics since the beginning because it is thought that whoever controls the artifact controls the nation.

Many people consider the well-known point break at lovely Arugam Bay, a moon-shaped curl of soft sand, to be the finest surf location in the nation. It is a small community with only a few hundred residents, and everything is dispersed along a single road that runs parallel to the shore.

Ella is a charming little hill village in Sri Lanka's Uva Province, in the Badulla district. As part of Sri Lanka tour packages, it is thought to be one of the island's most stunning locations to explore.
